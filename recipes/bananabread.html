Banana bread Recipe

Description

Recipe for a Vegan banana bread (oil-free, no refined sugar)

Ingredients

  • Ripe bananas
  • Flour
  • Almond butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Raisin agents

Steps

  1. In a single mixing bowl, mash bananas really well, add maple syrup and almond butter. Optionally, add a dash of lemon or lime juice to help with leavening.
  2. Mix wet ingredients really well. Slowly fold in all of the dry ingredients in two to three batches.
  3. Transfer the batter to a lined baking tin and decorate with an extra banana sliced in half.
  4. Bake, cool, enjoy.
